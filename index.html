About Us

Chi Logistics was established in 2014 as we recognized a growing need in the industry for individualized, reliable and modern transportation practices. We know the stories of unsatisfied drivers switching from company to company, so we decided to change that business model. Our team got together and recognized the needs of hard-working drivers; steady loads, higher rates, a reliable paycheck and to be treated as we would family.
